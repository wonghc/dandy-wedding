Accomodations

Black Canyon Inn

The venue is hosted in the Black Canyon Inn, a series of individual units with letter names (A-H). On the same property but a little further away is the Black Canyon Resort, a traditional hotel style setup. Staff driving golf carts will ferry guests living on the property to and from the venue on the day of the event. Book your room at the Black Canyon Inn.

Many other hotel options are located within a 10 minute drive from the venue.
